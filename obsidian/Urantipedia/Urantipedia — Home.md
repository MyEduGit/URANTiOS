# Urantipedia — Home

> [[URANTiOS — Home|↑ URANTiOS Home]]

**The encyclopedia of the Foreword and the 196 papers.**

> [!abstract] What Urantipedia is
> Each entry takes a concept or term — *consciousness*, *personality*,
> *the Supreme* — and examines it from every angle: its canonical
> definition, semantic roots, facets, ramifications, and the implicit
> meanings that are not directly evident. Every entry is anchored to its
> real appearances across all 197 papers, linked to the exact paragraph.

> [!tip] Add a concept
> Copy `_Concept Template`, rename it to the concept, then add the term
> to `SEED_CONCEPTS` in `pipeline/build_urantipedia.py` and re-run to
> harvest its appearances.
