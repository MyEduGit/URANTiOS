## Semantic Extraction
- **[T]** Deity is *personalizable* — it can be expressed as God (personality).
- **[T]** Deity is also *prepersonal* and *superpersonal*, in ways not fully comprehensible to humans.
- **[T]** Deity is characterized by the quality of **unity** — *actual or potential*.
- **[T]** This unity holds on *all supermaterial levels of reality*.
- **[T]** Creatures best comprehend this unifying quality as **divinity**.

## Alignment & Safety
- **Misinterpretation risks:** collapsing `Deity` into `God` (losing the prepersonal/superpersonal modes); treating `Divinity` as a substance rather than a perceived quality.
- **Dogma-drift risks:** hard-coding human-comprehensible (personal) Deity as the *whole* of Deity, quietly dropping the explicitly incomprehensible modes.
- **Mitigations:** keep `mode` an open enum with an explicit `not-fully-comprehensible` flag; bound creature readings with `fidelity < 1`; require human review before any change to the unity invariant.
